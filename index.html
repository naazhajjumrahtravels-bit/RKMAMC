About Our Institution

RKM Ayurved Medical
College & Hospital

RKM Ayurved Medical College & Hospital is a premier institution dedicated to the teaching, practice, and propagation of Ayurveda — the ancient Indian system of medicine. Established under the aegis of the RKM Trust, the college is approved by the National Commission for Indian System of Medicine (NCISM) and affiliated to a recognized university.

Our institution offers the Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS) programme, integrating classical Ayurvedic knowledge with contemporary medical sciences. With a 150+ bed attached hospital, well-equipped laboratories, a rich library, and a lush herbal garden, we provide a holistic learning environment for aspiring Ayurvedic physicians.

Our Vision

To be a centre of excellence in Ayurvedic education, research, and healthcare, producing globally competent Ayurvedic professionals.

Our Mission

To impart quality Ayurvedic education, provide affordable healthcare to the community, and promote research in traditional medicine.

Our Hospital

RKM Ayurved Hospital

Our 150+ bed teaching hospital provides authentic Ayurvedic treatments to the community while serving as the primary clinical training ground for BAMS students. With daily OPD, specialized Panchakarma therapies, and an in-house pharmacy, we deliver effective and affordable healthcare.

Panchakarma Therapy Unit

Vamana, Virechana, Basti, Nasya, Raktamokshana — performed by trained therapists under expert supervision. Separate male & female units.

OPD & IPD Services

Daily OPD across all 8 clinical specialties. Well-maintained IPD wards with round-the-clock nursing care and emergency services.

In-House Pharmacy (Rasashastra Lab)

Classical Ayurvedic formulations prepared on campus — Kwath, Avaleha, Asava-Arishta, Churna, Bhasma, and Kshara preparations.

How To Get Admitted

1

Eligibility Criteria

  • Qualified NEET UG in the relevant year
  • 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology
  • General: 50% aggregate in PCB; OBC/SC/ST: 40%
  • Age: Minimum 17 years as on 31st December of admission year
  • Indian citizen / OCI card holder eligible
2

Admission Process

  1. aRegister & appear for NEET UG exam
  2. bParticipate in State Counselling / AACCC Central Counselling
  3. cFill RKM as preference choice during choice filling
  4. dSeat allotment — report for document verification
  5. eReport to college with originals, pay fees, confirm admission
